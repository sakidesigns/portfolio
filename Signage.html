Project

Digital Signage

Echo Show 15 Screen

Overview

Miyajima Pharmacy has implemented digital signage to effectively communicate essential information to patients, including operating hours, available services, health tips, and the latest news. This initiative also ensures patients are informed about important changes in healthcare policies, such as the digitalization of health care cards, enhancing overall patient engagement.

To address the need for an effective communication tool, Miyajima Pharmacy has chosen the Amazon Echo Show 15, influenced by its cost-effectiveness, compact design, and versatile features. Measuring 15.8 inches diagonally, this affordable solution fits the pharmacy's budget and limited space, while also providing both informational displays and interactive capabilities.

Omotenashi Connect

View Live Site

Overview

Omotenashi Connect offers affordable translation and localization for Japanese tourism businesses. The responsive website features case studies and reviews, illustrating how poor translations deter foreign customers. It guides users through contacting the service, emphasizing the importance of quality localization to enhance customer experiences and business growth.

The Problem Statement


  • Language barriers hinder Japanese tourism businesses from effectively serving international tourists.

  • Limited access to affordable translations complicates providing localized experiences.

  • Lack of user-friendly platforms leaves business owners unsure about obtaining quality localization.

The Solution Statement


  • Omotenashi Connect educates on localization's business benefits, emphasizing its quick grasp on importance.

  • The site simplifies access to localization, highlighting its role in global appeal and satisfaction.

  • Through cases and reviews, it shows how localization improves user experience, urging businesses to invest.

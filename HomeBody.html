Project

HomeBody

Home Workout Assistant App

Overview

The project aims to create a fitness app that revolutionizes home workouts by utilizing common household items as weights, such as cans of soup, milk jugs, or cereal boxes. As a UX researcher, the goal is to understand how users approach working out at home and their struggles, then design creative solutions to enhance their home fitness experience. Target users include home exercisers, fitness enthusiasts, budget-conscious individuals, and busy users who prefer working out at home for convenience, affordability, and flexibility.

The Problem Statement



  • Limited access to gym equipment during challenging times like COVID-19 hinders consistent fitness routines.

  • Decreased physical activity levels due to the inability to visit gyms or engage in outdoor activities.

  • Lack of external motivation leads to difficulty staying committed to fitness goals.

  • Limited variety in home workouts contributes to decreased engagement and interest in exercising regularly.

The Solution Statement



  • Develop a user-friendly fitness app for home workouts during challenging times like COVID-19.

  • Utilize common household items as substitutes for gym equipment to offer a cost-effective fitness solution.

  • Integrate personalized workout plans, instructional videos, progress tracking, and motivational content.

  • Provide innovative and creative exercises for convenient home workouts without needing gyms or outdoor spaces.

Final Design

Below is the user flow for logging in or signing up for the first time. If it's a new user, the app will prompt them to create a profile to enable the algorithm to generate a personalized workout plan.

If the user chooses to create a profile, they will answer questions about their body, personal preferences, and workout habits. Based on this information, the app will generate a customized program that matches their needs.

If the user opts not to create a profile, they will be presented with an empty dashboard in the fitness app. The app will prompt them to add preset workout programs or exercises, which they can select individually.

From the dashboard, users can access workout videos and choose modified versions of each workout. Upon completing a workout, they will be directed to the workout log to track their accomplishments.

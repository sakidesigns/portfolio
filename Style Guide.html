Actions

  1. Sarah downloads the app from the app store.

  2. Sarah creates her profile, providing details about her travel interests, skills, and volunteering preferences.

  1. Sarah explores host profiles and locations in the app.

  2. She uses the search function to find hosts for volunteering.

  3. Sarah saves potential hosts to her favorites for later.

  1. Sarah sends messages to hosts, introducing herself and discussing volunteering tasks.

  2. She receives responses, clarifying details like accommodation availability and volunteering responsibilities.

  3. Sarah finalizes plans with a host, confirming stay dates and logistics.

  1. Sarah travels to the host's location.

  2. She settles in and begins volunteering, helping with community projects, cultural events, or language assistance.

  3. Sarah documents and shares her experiences on the app's community platform.
Opportunities for improvement

  • Create engaging onboarding to showcase volunteering benefits.

  • Provide a tutorial for effective profile setup.

  • Include videos highlighting user success stories.

  • Implement advanced search filters for users like Sarah.

  • Add a map view feature showing host locations and nearby attractions.

  • Create a recommendation system based on Sarah's past experiences.

  • Enhance messaging to support multimedia content like photos and videos for showcasing skills.

  • Add real-time notifications for milestones like booking confirmations and follow-up messages.

  • Provide templates for initial messages to hosts for smoother communication and coordination.

  • Provide additional resources and local information to help Sarah prepare for her trip.

  • Implement a feedback system for hosts and travelers to rate experiences and offer feedback.

  • Introduce virtual meetups in the app to foster connections beyond immediate stays.
